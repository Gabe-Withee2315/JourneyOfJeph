# JourneyOfJeph
A short game about a Tortoise rescuing his wife.

Jeph is a Galapagos Tortoise trying to rescue his wife Tortellina from evil Hares
Jeph will do this through brute force and several attacks he has access to:
1. Spin Move – A spinning attach
2. Chomp – A bite attack
3. Launch – A robotic attack where Jeph can propel himself at his enemies using a propulsion system built into his shell
sp attack 1. Bombshell – An attack where Jeph literally becomes a bomb

to complete his mission Jeph needs to fight through the hordes of Hares and defeat the king hare, Jack.
